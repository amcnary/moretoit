--- layout: page title: About Janea and Moreto It permalink: /about/ ---

Janea Moreto is a born and bred Angeleno, giving her wide exposure to not just fashion but also to a melting pot of cultures and peoples. She embraces this experience along with her in-depth industry knowledge as both a wardrobe and personal stylist.

"What I’ve fallen in love with most is the art of creating and engaging—the inspiration is never-ending."

Created as a visual space to join in the on-going fashion conversation, moreto it dares to bring a unique insight pushing further than just fashion. Because, well, there’s always more to it.

Janea currently lives and styles in Los Angeles.
